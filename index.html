About Me

My name is Max Jackson and I graduated from NAU in 2016 with my Master's Degree in Educational Leadership, CCHE (Community College and Higher Education). I began a doctorate in the same discipline/focus, but soon realized that web development is calling my name.


I began studying full stack web development in January, 2019 and I love every minute of it. No longer do I dread reading graduate-level theoretical text. Rather, I am eager to find free time to work on projects for my web development classes. Coding is better than any SSRI on the market!


I have been working for the University of Arizona for over twelve years. Currently, I am Coordinator of Admission and New Student Enrollment for the SALT Center. Please check out the rest of this portfolio and contact me if you'd like to network!
